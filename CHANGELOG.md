## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `LiquidGlassWidget` — single glass pane with blur, refraction, Fresnel, glare.
* `LiquidGlassBlobGroup` — multi-shape blob-merge renderer.
* Built-in presets: `appleDefault`, `subtle`, `bold`.
* `SpringSizeAnimation` and `LiquidGlassShimmerMixin` for animations.
* SDF utilities via `LiquidGlassShapes`.
